Hello!

I am a 1st year Ph.D. student studying Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC). My research has been heavily focused on studying the high-energy emissions from Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) and more specifically blazars~ (accreting supermassive black holes with jets pointed towards our line of sight). Since starting grad school, my research focus has expanded to using resolved stellar spectra to understand galaxy evolution in the nearby galaxies.
